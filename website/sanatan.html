The Eternal Truth

Sanatan Dharma

Sanatan Dharma is not a religion, but the eternal, universal law of reality. It encompasses the duties, cosmic order, and the spiritual path to ultimate liberation. It is the very fabric of existence, uncreated and eternal, mapping the journey of the soul through time and space.

Dharma

The cosmic law underlying right behavior and social order. It is the righteous path that maintains the harmony and balance of the universe.

Karma & Samsara

The universal principle of cause and effect, and the continuous cycle of birth, death, and rebirth driven by the soul's actions.

Moksha

The ultimate goal—liberation from the cycle of Samsara. A state of absolute freedom, realization, and union with the Supreme reality.

Vedic Vijnana

Explore the absolute sciences of the ancient Rishis. From the mathematics of time (Kala-Vijnana) to sacred astronomy, mathematically verified through modern planetary engines.
