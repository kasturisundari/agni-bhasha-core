1. Share Your Code

Each wallet gets a unique referral code. Share it with developers, researchers, and enthusiasts.

Referred members must reach 25 activity points (coding, ideas, voting, publishing) to count as "active".

When 10 referrals become active, a DAO proposal auto-triggers. The community votes to award your reward.
